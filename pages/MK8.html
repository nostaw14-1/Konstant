Mario Kart 8

The kart racing game Mario Kart 8 was created and released by Nintendo in May 2014 for the Wii U. It keeps the gameplay elements from the Mario Kart series, where players drive karts while controlling characters from the Mario franchise and collecting various objects to hinder rivals or gain an advantage. In addition to introducing anti-gravity racing over walls and ceilings, Mario Kart 8 now allows players to collide with one another for a quick boost. Both single-player and multiplayer options are available, including online play over the Nintendo Network. In April 2017, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe, an improved version for the Nintendo Switch, was made available. It was also a hit, with reviewers praising the extension of the battle mode, and it ended up being the Switch game with the highest sales. Mario Kart 8 has sold a total of more than 55 million copies on Wii U and Switch, making it one of the best-selling video games of all time.
