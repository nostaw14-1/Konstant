Super Mario Odyssey

Super Mario Odyssey is a 2017 action-adventure platform game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch video game console. An entry in the Super Mario series, it follows Mario and his new ally Cappy—a sentient hat that allows Mario to control other characters and objects—as they journey across various kingdoms to save Princess Peach from his nemesis Bowser's plans of forced marriage. In contrast to the linear gameplay of prior entries, the game returns to the primarily open-ended, 3D platform gameplay featured in Super Mario 64 and Super Mario Sunshine. Odyssey was released on October 27, 2017. Super Mario Odyssey is one of the most highly rated games, scoring a 97% on Metacritic, which is identical to games such as Red Dead Redemption, and Super Mario Galaxy. After only three days on the market, it had sold over 2 million copies, with now over 23 million sold worldwide.
