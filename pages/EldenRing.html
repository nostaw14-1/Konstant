Elden Ring

Elden Ring, a 2022 action roleplaying game was created by FromSoftware and released by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Hidetaka Miyazaki was the director, and fantasy author George R. R. Martin created the world. On February 25, it became available for the PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S. In Elden Ring, players command a player character who can be customised as they travel to fix the Elden Ring and take over as the new Elden Lord. Players are able to explore the game's dynamic open environment while viewing it from a third-person perspective. Horseback riding, crafting, and battle utilising a variety of weapons and magic spells are all included in the gameplay. FromSoftware wanted to create an open-world game with gameplay similar to their existing Dark Souls series, intending Elden Ring to act as an evolution of Dark Souls. Martin did excellent work, and Miyazaki anticipated that his input would result in a story that was easier to understand than in past FromSoftware productions. Critics complimented Elden Ring for its gameplay and scenery while criticising its stagnant challenge and poor technological performance. The game sold more than 16.6 million copies by July 2022.
